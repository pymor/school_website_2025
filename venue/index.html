Traveling to Zagreb

  • Zagreb International Airport (Franjo Tuđman Airport)
    • bus 290 leaves every 35min, and takes about 40min to reach Kvaternik square (Kvaternikov trg)
    • the other option is a taxi
  • Croatian Railways
    • the main train station is here
  • Zagreb Bus Terminal
    • the main bus station is here

Traveling within Zagreb

  • ZET
    • public transportation in Zagreb: trams and buses
    • Google Maps gives good directions
    • ticket prices are given here
    • it is possible to buy paper tickets from newspaper stands or from the driver (it needs to be stamped by inserting into the machine close to the first door of the tram/bus, or close to the first or last door in the case of low-floor trams)
    • instead of paper tickets, it is possible to use an app (a QR code needs to be scanned which appear close to the doors of the tram/bus)
  • Bolt
    • taxi service

Getting to the Department of Mathematics

  • note that it is on a hill
  • buses going from Kaptol:
    • all except 105 (alight at Bijenička bus station)
  • buses going from Kvaternik square:
    • 201 (alight at Horvatovac - PMF bus station)
    • 204 (alight at Bijenička bus station)
  • the main entrance is on the north side of the building
